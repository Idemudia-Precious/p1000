Project 1000....An Affiliation of Goal getters.
It's no news that the world is fast moving towards the digital technology, hence the need for every personal or business brand to adjust to the right terms and conditions for visibility, promotion, massive sales and global recognition; which is why Laciudad has deemed it fit to contribute our quota to make the world a better place.
Laciudad Inc. came up with the Project 1000 plan to promote both business and personal brands.
We are presenting an uncommon opportunity to selective personal and business brands all over the world to become Affiliate Partners for one of our Intellectual Properties–LACIUDAD NETWORK(www.laciudadnet.com).
We currently introduced the website, Laciudad Network with amazing features which will not only affect personal and business brands in a positive way but will also curb poverty in many lives.
Our Company hereby offers your brand an opportunity to become an affiliate partner.
This opportunity will not only increase your brand visibility, but it will also be a form of massive promotion which will bring about global recognition.
Why Partnership?
Partnership is crucial to the growth of any business venture. A Partnership is a legal agreement between two parties which would bring equal benefits to both parties.
Merchants and traders from time immemorial, have made use of the principle of a strategic partnership to conduct their businesses which has greatly helped increase their visibility and business promotion.
The trend is still very much applicable today. Few Samples are Xender partnering with tiktok, Facebook partnering with WooCommerce, amongst others.
These big brands do these because they understand how important, strategic and helpful partnering is even when they are big brands already. They understand the power of many when compared to one, which is why they keep signing partnerships.
This is why Laciudad is bringing an awesome opportunity to the the table for personal and business brands to sign a partnership agreement which would help promote and grow our brands simultaneously. It's a win-win business model where everyone is on the winning side.
Why partner with Laciudad?
The name "Laciudad" is a Spanish word which means "The City". The idea was to simply bring up a name which connotes a team of seasoned people with great minds, working together to produce invaluable global results which will solve problems of humanity and make the world a better place.
Laciudad Inc. is a digitalized business city that inspires, nurtures and grow ideas, resulting to positive impact on economic growth and the world at large.
Laciudad Inc. is currently registered under The Corporate Affairs Commision of Nigeria( CAC) and Certified by Ministry of Trade and Commerce.
